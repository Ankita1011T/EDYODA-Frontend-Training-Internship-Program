ANIMALS LOVERS

Cat lovers like being low-key, silent and calm whereas dog lovers are said to be more dramatic in their approach. It is a generalised thought indeed but as per the observation of several people we asked, dog people like to make a fuss sometimes and they voice out the smallest of their problems in most cases whereas cat lovers stifle their emotions until it gets too much like a lone wolf.
